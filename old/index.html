The Human Synthesizer

Horizontal hand position corresponds to pitch, vertical hand position corresponds to volume.

Webcam View

Information

Here you can make some music with your hands! If you are in reasonable lighting conditions and you hold up your hand to your camera, it will play a note with a frequency determined by the horizontal position and an amplitude determined by the vertical position. This is a little like a theremin which uses two antennae as the controls for an instrument.
